Opening doors for women.

Lei Wa Lakom is proud to announce that it's three seasons strong. We bring the untold stories of women whom have inspired us.

Opening Doors,
Discovering Possibilities

Here at Lei Wa Lakom, we bring you the stories of those who trailblazed their path in life. These women have challenged paradigms, broken glass ceilings, and redefined what it means to be a woman in our culture today.


By knowing what they have gone through, they become examples for us all to push the envelope. When it comes to discovering our true potential, we all could use inspiration to know what could be possible. This is the place for it.


Join us in our mission to make their voices heard and stories told,
